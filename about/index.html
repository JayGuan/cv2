Background

Coming from China with little English language skills in 2010, I have worked hard to overcome barriers. My family and friends told me that English skills can be obtained as time goes by, and I should not worry too much about it. Being an organized person, I always plan things out ahead of time. I started reading English articles, news, and novels daily. After a year, I started going out of my comfort zone to work in a restaurant where all employees were Americans. Through this job, I enhanced my language skills and helped the company to go through its employee shortage crisis. I also received a recommendation letter from my manager Christopher Staples. Here are the exact words from Mr. Staples, who has managed more than 500 employees in the past 20 years of his career, “I have never encountered a more dedicated, more earnest, and more committed employee.” Now, I am seeking opportunities to contribute my software development and information security skills.
